About Us

AgriConnect

The Agricultural Market Connector is a web app that links local farmers with urban buyers, featuring a marketplace for crops, price tracking, and delivery options. This innovative solution bridges the gap between rural agriculture and urban demand, enhancing local economies.

Tech Stack

Flask

Flask serves as the web framework for the application, enabling the development of a robust backend. It manages routing, handles requests, and provides a lightweight structure for building RESTful APIs, facilitating seamless communication between the frontend and backend.
